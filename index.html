Table Tennis Rules & Hints 2024

Rules

  • Players must serve the ball behind the end line.
  • The ball must bounce on your side before crossing the net.
  • Matches can be played as best of five or seven games.
  • Each game is won by the first player to reach 11 points.
  • Players switch sides after each game and in the deciding game after one player reaches 5 points.

Hints for Beginners

  • Practice your serve to gain an advantage.
  • Focus on footwork to position yourself better.
  • Work on your backhand and forehand equally.
  • Play regularly to build consistency.
  • Watch professional matches for technique and strategy.
